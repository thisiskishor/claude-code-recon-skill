## Inspiration & References

This skill was inspired by architectural thinking and domain wisdom principles from:

### acidgreenservers

- **GitHub:** https://github.com/acidgreenservers
- **Source:** [Domain Wisdom Gist](https://gist.github.com/acidgreenservers/001185d63e5cd65f9fbe6f7a1c70a200)

**What we learned:**

The **20 "domain wisdom seeds"** approach to identifying structural problems in production systems. Specifically:

- The seed concept itself (condensed architectural principles as memorable metaphors)
- The methodology for assessing architecture quality through proven patterns
- The principle-based risk categorization (understanding not just "what's wrong" but "why it matters")
- How to link technical findings back to underlying system design principles

This foundational thinking directly shaped the **Domain Wisdom Seeds** section and the **Risk Summary** approach in code-recon's Standard and Deep scan modes.
